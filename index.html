YEARS OF SP!

About SP70


2024 marks the 70th year of Singapore's first polytechnic, Singapore Polytechnic. Throughout these 70 years, SP have grown to become the largest polytechnic in Singapore with more than 230,000 graduates produced. SP70 is an event to celebrate the milestone and the beginning of the next era of SP.

Timeline

1954

On 27 October, the Legislative Council passed the Singapore Ordinance, which led to the establishment of Singapore's first polytechnic.

1959

Official opening of Singapore Polytechnic at Prince Edward Road by Prince Philip, the Duke of Edinburgh on 24 February.

1979

Dover Road campus officially opened by Prime Minister Lee Kuan Yew on 7 July and it is chosen as the permanent home of Singapore Polytechnic.

2000

100,000th graduate received his diploma at the 40th Graduation Ceremony

2010

SP organised RoboCup 2010, a world-class event dubbed the Olympics of the Robotics world. It was the first time the event was held in Singapore.
